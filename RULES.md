# Buildathon Rules

## Goal

Build an office entrance greeting system that continuously plays videos fullscreen while detecting known people through a camera and showing a greeting overlay.

## Core Requirements

- The video playlist must loop continuously in fullscreen.
- Face detection must run while the video continues playing.
- Known people must trigger a greeting overlay.
- Unknown people must not interrupt the video.
- The system must support adding and removing known people from the database.
- The submitted project must include setup instructions.

## Submission Rules

- Submit your work through a pull request.
- Put your full project inside `submissions/your-name/`.
- Do not modify another participant's folder.
- Do not push directly to `main`.
- Include a demo video link that is 1-3 minutes long.
- Submit before the final deadline announced by the organizers.

## Judging Criteria

- Correctness of the required behavior
- Smoothness of the user experience
- Reliability of face detection and greeting logic
- Simplicity of setup
- Code organization and documentation
- Practicality for real office usage

## Allowed Technology

Any programming language, framework, or library is allowed unless organizers announce a restriction.

## Disqualification

A submission may be rejected if it:

- does not run or cannot be reviewed
- is submitted outside the participant's own folder
- is missing setup instructions
- is missing a demo video link
- includes malicious code
- copies another participant's work without permission
